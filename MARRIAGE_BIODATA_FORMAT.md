# Marriage Biodata Format Guide

## Standard Marriage Biodata Structure

A complete marriage biodata should include the following sections in order:

---

## 1. **HEADER SECTION**
- **Title**: "Marriage Biodata" or "विवाह बायोडाटा"
- **Profile Photo**: Professional, clear photograph (optional but recommended)
- **Full Name**: Complete name as it appears on official documents

---

## 2. **PERSONAL DETAILS**

### Basic Information
- **Full Name**: [Your complete name]
- **Date of Birth**: [DD/MM/YYYY]
- **Time of Birth**: [HH:MM AM/PM] (Optional but important for horoscope matching)
- **Place of Birth**: [City, State]
- **Age**: [Years]
- **Height**: [Feet & Inches or CM]
- **Weight**: [KG] (Optional)
- **Complexion**: Fair / Wheatish / Brown / Dark
- **Blood Group**: A+ / B+ / O+ / AB+ / etc.
- **Physical Status**: Normal / Physically Challenged

### Marital & Religious Information
- **Marital Status**: Never Married / Divorced / Widowed
- **Mother Tongue**: Hindi / English / Regional Language
- **Religion**: Hindu / Muslim / Christian / Sikh / Jain / Buddhist
- **Caste**: [Your caste]
- **Sub Caste**: [If applicable]
- **Gotra**: [If applicable, especially for Hindu marriages]
- **Manglik**: Yes / No / Partial (Anshik Manglik)

---

## 3. **EDUCATION & PROFESSION**

### Educational Background
- **Highest Qualification**: B.Tech / MBA / M.Sc / Ph.D / etc.
- **Field of Study**: Computer Science / Business / Medicine / etc.
- **Institute/University Name**: IIT / IIM / University Name
- **Year of Completion**: [Year]

### Professional Details
- **Profession/Occupation**: Software Engineer / Doctor / Business Owner / etc.
- **Company Name**: [Current company]
- **Designation**: Senior Developer / Manager / Director / etc.
- **Annual Income**: [LPA or Package]
- **Work Location**: [City, State]
- **About Profession**: Brief description of your career, achievements, and future plans

---

## 4. **FAMILY DETAILS**

### Parents Information
- **Father's Name**: [Full name]
- **Father's Occupation**: Business / Service / Retired / etc.
- **Mother's Name**: [Full name]
- **Mother's Occupation**: Homemaker / Teacher / Business / Service / etc.

### Family Background
- **Siblings**: 
  - Brothers: [Number] (Married/Unmarried)
  - Sisters: [Number] (Married/Unmarried)
- **Family Type**: Joint Family / Nuclear Family
- **Family Status**: Middle Class / Upper Middle Class / Affluent
- **Family Values**: Traditional / Modern / Liberal / Conservative
- **About Family**: Brief description of family background, values, and traditions

---

## 5. **HOROSCOPE DETAILS** (Optional but Important for Hindu Marriages)

- **Rashi (Zodiac Sign)**: Mesha / Vrishabha / Mithuna / etc.
- **Nakshatra (Birth Star)**: Ashwini / Bharani / Kritika / etc.
- **Gan**: Deva / Manushya / Rakshasa
- **Nadi**: Adi / Madhya / Antya
- **Charan**: 1st / 2nd / 3rd / 4th
- **Birth Star**: [If different from Nakshatra]
- **Horoscope Match**: Required / Not Required
- **Additional Horoscope Info**: Any other relevant astrological details

---

## 6. **LIFESTYLE & PREFERENCES**

### Lifestyle
- **Diet**: Vegetarian / Non-Vegetarian / Eggetarian / Jain Vegetarian
- **Drinking**: Never / Socially / Regularly
- **Smoking**: Never / Occasionally / Regularly

### Personal Interests
- **Hobbies**: Reading, Traveling, Music, Sports, etc.
- **Interests**: [Your interests and passions]
- **About Me**: Brief self-description, personality traits, values, and what makes you unique

### Partner Expectations
- **Partner Preferences**: What you're looking for in a life partner (optional but helpful)

---

## 7. **CONTACT INFORMATION**

### Primary Contact
- **Mobile Number**: [10-digit number]
- **Alternate Number**: [If available]
- **Email**: [Your email address]
- **WhatsApp Number**: [If different from mobile]

### Address Details
- **Residential Address**: [Complete address]
- **City**: [Your city]
- **State**: [Your state]
- **Pincode**: [Postal code]
- **Contact Person**: [Name of person to contact if you're unavailable]

---

## **FORMATTING GUIDELINES**

### Design Principles
1. **No Horizontal Lines**: Use decorative borders, background boxes, or simple spacing instead
2. **Elegant Typography**: Bold headings with proper spacing
3. **Cultural Elements**: Include traditional symbols, decorative borders, or cultural motifs
4. **Professional Layout**: Clean, organized, easy to read
5. **Color Scheme**: Use warm, traditional colors (red, gold, orange) for traditional templates

### Section Organization
- Use clear section headings
- Group related information together
- Maintain consistent spacing
- Use decorative elements instead of harsh lines
- Keep it visually appealing and culturally appropriate

### Best Practices
- ✅ Be honest and accurate
- ✅ Use professional language
- ✅ Include a good quality photo
- ✅ Keep descriptions concise but informative
- ✅ Proofread for spelling and grammar
- ✅ Use appropriate cultural elements
- ❌ Avoid too many horizontal lines
- ❌ Don't make it look like a resume
- ❌ Don't use overly corporate designs

## **IMPORTANT NOTES**

1. **Cultural Sensitivity**: Different communities may have different requirements
   - Hindu: Gotra, Manglik, Horoscope details are important
   - Muslim: Religious practices, family background
   - Christian: Church affiliation, family values
   - Sikh: Family traditions, religious practices

2. **Privacy**: Only share necessary information. Be cautious with personal details.

3. **Photo Guidelines**: 
   - Professional, clear photo
   - Recent (within 1-2 years)
   - Good lighting
   - Appropriate attire
   - Smiling, friendly expression

4. **Language**: Can be in English, Hindi, or regional languages depending on preference

---

This format ensures your marriage biodata is complete, professional, and culturally appropriate while maintaining elegance and avoiding overly corporate designs.
